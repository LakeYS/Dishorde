# Times for reference
In the game's console, use `settime` and `gettime` to test different times for 7d!time.

At the time of writing, the horde starts at 22:00 on Day 7 and ends at 06:00 on Day 8.

## Useful timestamps for testing
* Day 5, 04:00 - 100000
* Day 6, 20:00 - 140000
* Day 7, 06:00 - 150000
* Day 7, 21:00 - 165000
* Day 7, 23:30 - 167500
* Day 8, 02:00 - 170000
* Day 8, 06:30 - 174500
